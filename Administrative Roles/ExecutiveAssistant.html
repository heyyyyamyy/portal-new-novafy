Executive Assistant

1: What do you believe are the key responsibilities of an Executive Assistant (EA)?

Answer: An Executive Assistant plays a crucial role in providing high-level administrative support to executives, managing schedules, handling correspondence, coordinating meetings, and ensuring the smooth functioning of daily operations.
